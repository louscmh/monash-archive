MORE THAN JUST A NAME.

Sonamu is the Korean name of the Red Pine Tree of Korea, Japan and adjacent parts of China and Russia. In Korea it has a special status, and is held to embody faithfulness, fortitude, and the Korean spirit. So come and visit us today, and experience the flavors of Korea right here in Canberra. We look forward to serving you!
